Based in the heart of Luton, Billups Studio is where culture meets clean sound. From quick bars to full tape lock-ins, this is the studio where your best work comes alive.

Our Luton-based recording studio delivers expert engineering, mixing, and mastering services for emerging and established artists. With industry-standard equipment and a dedicated team of producers, we help you achieve professional-grade sound every time.

No gimmicks. Just professional setup, local roots, and that unmatched vibe.

Pro Sound

Industry-standard equipment with pristine acoustic treatment for radio-ready recordings. Professional mixing and mastering included with every session.

Clean Space

Immaculate, inspiring environment designed for creativity and focus without distractions. Professional grade acoustic treatment throughout.

Good People

Experienced engineers who understand artists and know how to bring out your best performance. Direct contact with producers jaidee & dsoul.
